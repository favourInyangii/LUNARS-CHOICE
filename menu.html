LUNAR'S CHOICE RESTAURANT

Appetizers

Baked sweet Potato

Baked sweet potatoes are a good source of vitamins, minerals, and fiber, making them a healthy choice.

Fish fingers

Fish fingers are a popular finger food made from breaded and fried fish fillets, often served with tartar sauce or ketchup.

Pancakes

Pancakes are a breakfast staple made from a batter of flour, eggs, milk, and butter, cooked on a griddle and typically served with syrup or fruit toppings.

Main Course

Africa jollof rice

Jollof rice is a flavorful West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a blend of spices, often served with meat or vegetables.

Tortellini

Tortellini are small, stuffed pasta shapes that are typically filled with cheese, meat, or vegetables and served with sauce.

Rack of lamb

Rack of lamb is a cut of meat from the rib section of a lamb, usually roasted or grilled and served as a gourmet dish.

Desserts

Strawberry crush

From strawberry shortcakes to strawberry tarts, there are a variety of ways to incorporate this sweet and juicy fruit into desserts

Ice cream sundae

An ice cream sundae is a classic dessert made with scoops of ice cream topped with syrup, whipped cream, nuts, and a cherry.

Mango Cocco E

Mango coco E is a tropical dessert made with mango, coconut milk, and other ingredients, creating a sweet and refreshing treat.
